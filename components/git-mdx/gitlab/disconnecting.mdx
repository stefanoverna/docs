To disconnect your personal account or team on <ProductName /> from GitLab, take the following steps:

First, [click here](https://gitlab.com/profile/applications) to go to the Applications Settings page on GitLab.

Then, scroll to the bottom of the page and locate **<GitlabAppName /> + GitLab** and click the **Revoke** button next to it.

  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/uninstall-gitlab.png`}

<Caption>Revoking access.</Caption>

After disconnecting, you can always reconnect your <ProductName /> account to a GitLab account again.
