# Introduction

## <NameWrapper name={ORG_NAME} /> Integrations

&#8203;<OrgName /> Integrations introduce a way to **extend the capabilities of <OrgName />** and to connect with **any platform**.

The following is just some the things you can do with <OrgName /> Integrations:

- Add databases into deployments with just a few clicks.
- Add custom UI elements to the [<OrgName /> Dashboard](/).
- Complete access to the [<OrgName /> API](/docs/api).
- Configure third-party services such as error tracking and performance monitoring.
- Create mashup apps by connecting multiple different services of your choosing.
- Access hardware and connect with external devices such Arduino, Philips Hue, etc.

These are **just a few of the possibilities** afforded by <OrgName /> Integrations. This page will walk you through on how to create your own to do more with <OrgName />.

<Note>
  This page is dedicated to explaining what Integrations are and how you can
  develop your own. To install an Integration instead, go to the{' '}
  <Link href="/integrations">Integrations Marketplace</Link>.
</Note>
