To disconnect your personal account or team on <ProductName /> from Bitbucket, take the following steps:

First, log into [Bitbucket](https://bitbucket.org/), click on your profile icon on the bottom left, and select **Bitbucket settings**.

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/bitbucket-settings.png`}
  width={407 / 2}
  height={451 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>Bitbucket Settings.</Caption>

Next, from **Access Management**, select **OAuth**.

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/bitbucket-oauth.png`}
  width={449 / 2}
  height={242 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>OAuth Settings.</Caption>

Finally, from **OAuth integrated applications**, locate **<BitbucketAppName />** and click the **Revoke** link next to it.

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/uninstall-bitbucket.png`}
  width={934 / 2}
  height={374 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>Revoking access.</Caption>

After disconnecting, you can always reconnect your <ProductName /> account to a Bitbucket account again.
